Design Style Guide

The system the Auri website and product share, current as of v8: one type family (Geist), tabular numerals in place of monospace, pressed-plum as the single action accent, gold reserved for the asset — plus the v8 additions documented below: the device-in-card pattern, the live MCP·CLI·API terminal surface, amber-led risk/leverage (Boost), and the copy voice. Full data: brand/STYLE-GUIDE.md.

Philosophy

Six principles the product applies everywhere.

Calm, light canvas

Near-white lavender-gray, white cards, 1px hairlines. Color only where it carries meaning.

Numbers are tabular, never mono

Balances, prices, dates set in Geist with tabular figures — they align in columns without the typewriter texture.

Plum acts, gold is the asset

Plum = every action & wayfinding. Gold = the asset & its proof. Green = money-in/safe · Amber = caution & leverage · Red = loss.

One family: Geist

Display headlines at 620–660 with tight tracking; body at 450; every numeral tabular. No serif, no monospace.

Quiet elevation, one scale

Three steps: rest · raised · overlay. Real depth only on transient layers — menus, modals, the FAB.

One dark surface for power

Only the programmable panels (Automate · MCP · CLI · API) go near-black. Dark = developer power; light = money.

Risk & leverage (Boost)

Leverage is the one place amber leads. Every Boost surface pairs a Higher-risk tag with a plain-language liquidation line — never an uncaveated upside claim.

⚠ Higher risk Up to 5× leverage Amplifies losses · liquidation
The risk line is mandatory. Boost multiplies the upside — and the downside, with real liquidation risk. Borrow and Boost surfaces never claim "keep every gram" without it. AA-safe inks on the light pill: amber text #8A5E0F, loss text #B23131; on the dark code surface amber brightens to #E6B450.

Voice & copy

The product should read like a person who has done the thing — not a feature list. Four rules; every string obeys them.

Lead with the moment, not the mechanism.✓ "Send money home before you finish texting."
✗ "Auri provides cross-border remittance."
One concrete detail beats three adjectives.Name the kettle, the ₹, the 30 seconds. Ban seamless · effortless · revolutionary · powerful.
Name what the old world can't do.Self-custody · no credit check · gift real grams to anyone · 24/7 · gold you can actually spend and leverage.
Say it once, never past what we can prove."attested" for gold, never "audited." Rates illustrative & variable. Borrow & Boost always carry the risk line.

Motion

Forward-only, one signature ease. Four durations, no springs — motion should feel decisive, not decorative.

TokenSpecUsed for
micro160ms · easeHover, press, focus, toggles
elem260ms · easeSheets, menus, tab swaps
enter420ms · easeContent entrances (fade-up)
reveal700ms · easeSignature: the dawn transition, gold pour

Ease everywhere: cubic-bezier(.22, 1, .36, 1) — a single decisive-out curve replaces the six ad-hoc timings.
