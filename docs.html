01Getting started

What Auri is. Auri is a gold neobank: buy real, allocated gold, then borrow, spend, send, gift and auto-invest it — all while it stays in your own custody. Auri is a software platform and never holds your funds.

Create your account

  1. Open the Auri app or web app and sign in — no password. Auth is passwordless, powered by Privy: continue with email, phone, Google, Apple, or a passkey.
  2. A non-custodial wallet only you control is created for you automatically — Auri holds $0 of your money.
  3. To fund from a Canadian bank or withdraw to one, complete a one-time identity check (KYC), handled by a licensed partner.

What you'll see

Your balance in CAD, with g / oz / XAUT units a tap away, a live gold price, and a Value / Returns toggle to switch between what your gold is worth and how it has performed. Core actions sit up front: Buy, Borrow, Send, Gift, Add cash. You never touch tokens, addresses, or gas.

Real gold, from the first gram. Every balance is backed 1:1 by Tether Gold (XAUT) — allocated, London Good Delivery bars vaulted in Switzerland and attested quarterly by BDO Italia. See Security & custody for the proof.

02Buying & selling gold

Buy gold

  1. Tap Buy gold.
  2. Choose your source: your cash balance, or fund directly by Interac e-Transfer, bank transfer (EFT), or debit card.
  3. Enter an amount in CAD or in gold (g / oz / XAUT). Start small — sub-gram amounts are fine.
  4. Review the price and any fee, then confirm. Your gold lands in your wallet, typically in about 30 seconds.

Sell gold

Tap Sell gold, enter an amount, review, confirm. Proceeds land in your cash balance, ready to withdraw or reuse.

Under the hood (optional). Your CAD is converted to USDC, then swapped for XAUT (Tether Gold) on Arbitrum. Swaps are gasless and batched — you never pay a network fee or hold a "gas" token. Each unit of XAUT is backed 1:1 by allocated physical gold in a Swiss vault.

03Cash & funding

Add cash

  • Interac e-Transfer — send from your Canadian bank; your cash balance updates on receipt.
  • Bank transfer (EFT) & debit card — where supported by our licensed partners.
  • Deposit crypto — send USDC to your Auri deposit address (more assets supported over time).

Withdraw cash

Withdraw to your bank account by Interac, or to a crypto wallet you control.

How your cash is held. Your cash balance is shown in Canadian dollars and held as USDC, a fully-reserved US-dollar stablecoin, in your own wallet. It is not a bank deposit and is not CDIC-insured.

04Borrowing against gold

How it works. Your gold is collateral for an instant credit line on Morpho. Borrow cash at 3.75% APR and withdraw to your bank — no credit check, no fixed schedule, repay whenever. Your gold stays yours, keeping its full upside.

Borrow, repay, unlock

  1. Tap Borrow. Use the slider to choose an amount; your loan-to-value (LTV) and liquidation price update live.
  2. Confirm at 3.75% APR. Cash lands in your balance, ready to withdraw.
  3. Repay any amount, anytime, from cash — your LTV drops as you do.
  4. Unlock collateral once repaid, to free your gold from the loan and use it elsewhere.
RiskBorrowing carries liquidation risk. If the value of your gold falls far enough against your loan, some of your gold can be automatically sold to repay it. Keep your LTV conservative and watch your live loan-health indicator. This is not investment or tax advice.
Under the hood (optional). Loans run on Morpho — immutable, open-source lending markets with $10B+ in deposits, the same rails major fintechs use. Auri's gold-loan market is public and traceable in real time: $248.6M originated to date.

05The Auri card

Spend straight from your gold, anywhere the card is accepted — tap, chip, or online. At checkout your gold converts to local currency; you spend gold, the merchant gets paid normally. Gold markets close on weekends — your card doesn't: it's open 24/7 · 365.

  • Freeze / unfreeze the card in one tap.
  • Set spend limits and review converted transactions — each purchase shows its cost in gold and in local currency.
  • Card settings — manage details, limits, and security.

06Gifting & remittance

Gift gold

Give real gold for any occasion — Birthday, Wedding, Festival, Shagun, Congrats, or Just because.

  1. Choose an amount and occasion, and add a message.
  2. Deliver via Contact, a Share link, or a QR code — reach the recipient by mobile or email.
  3. They claim it as real gold — no Auri account needed to start.

Send gold

Send gold to anyone in seconds — to a contact, an @username, or a wallet address. The recipient claims it as gold, or it lands directly in a wallet you specify. No wrapping, just a transfer.

Remit abroad

  1. Choose the destination country; the receiving currency is set automatically (e.g., India → INR).
  2. Enter the amount — you'll see a live, mid-market conversion and the recipient's exact payout.
  3. Add the recipient's bank details and send. They receive local currency in their bank account, often in minutes, through our partner rails.

07Auto-invest & Boost

Auto-invest (SIP / DCA)

Stack gold on autopilot. Set a recurring buy — weekly, on payday, or monthly — and Auri dollar-cost-averages for you. Track your average buy price, pause anytime, and pay no setup fee.

  1. Tap Auto-invest, set an amount and frequency (weekly / payday / monthly).
  2. Run multiple plans at once (e.g., CA$25 weekly + CA$100 on payday).
  3. Pause, edit, or cancel anytime.

Boost (advanced — leverage)

Boost adds up to 5× exposure to gold in one tap. Built for pros who want the throttle and know it cuts both ways.

Higher riskLeverage cuts both ways. Boost amplifies losses as much as gains, and positions can be liquidated — you can lose more than holding gold outright. Only Boost what you can afford to lose. Not investment advice; see the Risk Disclosure.

08MCP & CLI quickstart

Automate puts the same attested, self-custodied gold behind MCP · CLI · API keys — drive Auri from Claude, Codex, or any agent, and script gold strategies from your shell.

Install

(Package names are placeholders until public release.)

# npm
$ npm install -g @auri/cli

# or Homebrew
$ brew install auri

Authenticate

$ auri login
# Opens your browser, signs in with Privy (passkey),
# and stores a scoped API token locally.
✓ signed in · scoped token saved to ~/.auri

Connect the MCP server to your agent

# Auto-configure a known client
$ auri mcp install --client claude   # or: --client codex
✓ connected · tools: buy, sell, borrow, repay, send, gift, boost

Or add it to your MCP config manually:

{
  "mcpServers": {
    "auri": { "command": "auri", "args": ["mcp"] }
  }
}

Example agent prompts

  • “What's my gold balance and current LTV?”
  • “Buy CA$50 of gold every Friday and keep my average price.”
  • “Borrow CA$200 against my gold, but never let my LTV cross 40%.”
  • “Send CA$25 in gold to @arjun.”

Example CLI commands

$ auri balance
$ auri price --unit oz --currency CAD
$ auri buy --gold 50 --every week      # recurring buy · SIP
$ auri borrow --cad 200
$ auri send --to @arjun --gold 25
$ auri boost --leverage 5x --confirm   # advanced · liquidation risk
Good to know. A sandbox is available for testing. Every action respects your account limits and the same verification and risk controls as the app — including liquidation risk on borrowing and Boost.

09API overview

10Security & custody

  • Self-custody by design. Your gold and cash live in a wallet you control. Auri never takes custody — Auri holds $0 of your funds.
  • Passwordless auth. Sign-in is handled by Privy — email, phone, Google, Apple, or passkey. No passwords to phish or leak.
  • Licensed partners. Identity checks (KYC) and fiat on/off-ramps are run by licensed third parties, so the regulated steps stay with regulated firms.
  • Attested reserves. Your gold is Tether Gold (XAUT) — 707,747 oz of fine gold (22+ tonnes) across 1,792 London Good Delivery bars, vaulted in Switzerland and attested quarterly by BDO Italia. Verify the exact bars behind your wallet anytime.
  • Not a bank. Auri is a non-custodial software platform, not a bank or deposit-taking institution. Balances are not deposits and are not CDIC-insured. Gold, crypto, borrowing, and leverage all carry risk.
Proof, not promises. Reserves are attested quarterly (latest as of March 31, 2026) and your holdings are verifiable on-chain — check the vault and your own allocation whenever you like.
