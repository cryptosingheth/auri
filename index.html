Fair questions, straight answers.

Eight that matter most. The long tail — fees, taxes, regions, security — lives in the docs.

Is my gold real?+
Yes — every balance is Tether Gold (XAUT): real, allocated London Good Delivery bars in Swiss vaults, attested every quarter by BDO Italia. You can trace your holdings down to the individual numbered bar, anytime — it's gold you own, not an IOU or a price feed.
Does Auri hold my money?+
No — Auri holds $0. Your gold and cash live in a wallet only you control, secured by passkeys. If Auri disappeared tomorrow, your gold would still be yours, provable on a public ledger.
I'm not into crypto. Is this complicated?+
Not at all. You sign in with just an email, phone or passkey — no password, ever — fund by Interac, and pay in Canadian dollars. View everything in CAD, grams, ounces or XAUT; the machinery stays under the hood.
What's the catch with borrowing?+
Borrowing is instant with no credit check, at 3.75% APR — but it carries liquidation risk: if gold falls far enough against your loan, part of your gold can be sold to repay it. Your LTV and liquidation price are on screen live, so borrow conservatively and keep an eye on them.
Is Auri a bank? Is my money insured?+
No — Auri is a non-custodial software platform, not a bank, and balances aren't deposits or CDIC-insured. What you get instead is custody: your gold and cash never touch our balance sheet — they sit in your wallet.
What does it cost?+
The exact price, rate and any fee appear on the confirm screen before every action — no hidden spreads, no storage fees, no surprises. Auto-Invest carries no fee, and you can pause it anytime.
Can I spend, send and gift my gold?+
Yes. The Auri card spends straight from your gold — tap, chip or online, any day of the week. Send gold to a contact, @username or wallet, gift it for a birthday, wedding or Diwali with the occasion built in, or remit it abroad — all in minutes.
What do I get for using Auri?+
Auto-Invest quietly stacks gold on your schedule — weekly, payday or monthly — averaging your price with no fee. You collect Auri Jewels as you go (redeem for gift cards, fee waivers and partner deals), and Refer & earn pays you in real gold for every friend who joins.

Digital assets — including tokenized gold and stablecoins — are not deposits and are not insured by the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation (CDIC) or any government deposit-insurance scheme. The value of gold and crypto assets can go down as well as up, and you can lose money.
